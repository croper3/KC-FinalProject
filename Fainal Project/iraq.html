The secret of discovering the Pythagorean theorem

The ancient Babylonians understood the Pythagorean theorem more than a thousand years before the birth of the Greek philosopher Pythagoras.

Why is it called Mesopotamia?

Mesopotamia was called by this name because of the emergence of its great civilization on the banks of the Tigris and Euphrates rivers.

The Iraqi invasion of Iran

The Iraqi invasion of Iran began on September 22 and lasted until December 7, 1980. This invasion led to an eight-year war between Iran and Iraq.
